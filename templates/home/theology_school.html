Our Mission

We aim to provide a transformative educational experience that prepares leaders who are academically proficient, compassionate, ethical, and socially responsible. Rooted in the rich tradition of the Consolata Missionaries, we foster a learning community where intellectual curiosity is encouraged, spiritual growth is nurtured, and a passion for mission and service is ignited.

Spiritual Formation & Guidance

The School of Theology offers opportunities for regular spiritual services, prayer groups, retreats, and personal spiritual direction. These experiences nurture the spiritual lives of students, helping them grow in faith and deepen their relationship with God.

Mentorship & Vocation Guidance

Faculty members often serve as mentors, guiding students on their personal faith journeys and helping them reflect deeply on their vocational call. This mentorship plays a crucial role in the holistic formation of students.

Why Study at CIU School of Theology?

  • Education: Rooted in the philosophy of Catholic Higher Education with a special focus on Mission Studies.
  • Holistic Formation: Integration of rigorous theological education with personal spiritual development for well-rounded leadership.
  • Diverse Programs: Flexible courses designed for pastoral ministry, academic research, and community leadership.
  • Global Perspective: A cross-cultural approach to theology that enhances ministry, outreach, and missionary service.
